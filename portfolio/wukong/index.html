After successfully escorting Tripitaka and retrieving the Buddhist scriptures, Sun Wukong ascends the mountain, but eventually rejects immortality
in order to live in peace on Mount Huaguo. However, the Heavenly Palace is furious that Sun Wukong does not follow their orders, and sends an
army led by Yirangjinjun to force him to return to the court. Sun Wukong duels with Yirangjinjun, but is ultimately defeated and sealed in a stone.
However, just before he is sealed, Sun Wukong divides his power into six roots—six souls—and hides them.

Over the next several centuries, the monkeys of Mount Huaguo send many young warriors to retrieve the relic so that they can
resurrect and free Sun Wukong. One of those monkeys, Tianmingzi, begins his adventure in search of the relic

Staff Stances

There are three Staff Stances in Black Myth: Wukong.
They are Smash Stance, Pillar Stance and Thrust Stance.
Each of the stances have their own skill tree that costs Sparks to upgrade and enhances the stance.
There is also a Talent tree that can enhance other aspects of the combat.
